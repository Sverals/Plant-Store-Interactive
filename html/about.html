About Us

Our Mission

Shirley Good Growing Co seeks to connect people through their love of gardening. If you are looking to get into gardening, or you already have experience, our team of experienced gardeners will be able to help you with all your gardening needs from our greenhouse, or partner greenhouses.

Additional Information

When did we open?

Shirley Good Growing Co. first opened in 2004, where we have expanded to other locations and partner with other local greenhouses.

What products do we offer?

We sell potted plants ranging from vegetables to fruit trees. We also sell garden accessories and landscaping materials.

What services do you offer?

We contract with many partners to offer material delivery and landscaping.

Where are you located?

Our primary store is located in Atlanta Georgia, although we are connected to other greenhouses across the country to deliver everywhere in the United States.
